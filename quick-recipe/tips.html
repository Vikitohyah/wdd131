🍳Quick Cooking Hacks

  • Use pre-cut veggies to save time.
  • One-pot meals reduce cleanup!
  • Freeze sauces in ice cube trays.
  • Keep garlic in the freezer; it is easier to peel and lasts longer.
  • Use a muffin tin for meal prep; perfect portion control for freezing meals.
  • Boil water faster; cover the pot with a lid to speed it up.
  • Soak onions in water; removes the sour taste before serving raw.
  • Plan meals ahead; reduces impulse eating and saves time during the week.

🥗Healthy Eating Tips

  • Choose whole grains over refined carbs.
  • Batch cook for easy weekday meals.
  • Snack on fruits and nuts instead of chips.
  • Choose healthy oils; olive oil and avocado oil are great heart-healthy options.
  • Eat the rainbow; variety in fruit and veggie colors = variety in nutrients.
  • Use Greek yogurt; it is a healthy substitute for sour cream or mayonnaise.
  • Swap soda for infused water; add citrus, berries, or cucumber for flavor.
